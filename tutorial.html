[Tutorial]

[RULES]

  1. Wait until the number appear, then click where Yo want to place it.
  2. Place your number in any position to form a square, vertical line, horizontal line
    of 4 numbers.
  3. If The largest number between the 4 elements is divible by the other 3, the four slots will
    be emptied and You'll receive a certain score.
  4. You loose when you fill all the slots

  5. ex: Place the number 5 in 2x2, 5,1 and 10 divise 10, so those 4 slots will be emptied.
